    Asked Questions

    Frequently asked questions about visualizer

    • How does Riyadvi360 create a VR room ?

      Wizart ensures high-quality product visualization in a few clicks and provides your customers with a sense of purchase before buying and get rid of the imagination gap problem.

    • How long does it take to integrate VR Visualizer?

      The time needed for our team to prepare all the content depends on many factors, including the quality of the original data, the complexity of the product texture, etc. On average, you can expect to get a final VR room for one product within 1 week.

    • Are there any hardware or software requirements for the integration of the VR visualizer?

      No, the Wizart VR visualizer doesn’t require any additional equipment rather than an electronic device with access to the internet. It works with every CMS out there and can be seamlessly integrated into any website.

    • Is it possible to get a custom interior for the VR room?

      Yes, If you are interested in creating a custom interior, our team will be happy to accommodate your needs. Just contact us at sales@wizart.ai to request a custom quote.

    • Can I rotate the tiles placed by me in the tile visualizer?

      Yes, you can rotate the tiles in the horizontal or vertical direction, placed by you, in the pre-loaded rooms.

    • Can I save my final room design as an image?

      Yes, you can save the room layout as an image in JPEG, JPG, and PNG format.
